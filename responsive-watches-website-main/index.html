Company History

BAYLEAF is a partnership entity created by four friends ( Lakshya G, Niryukha B, Sudhiksha K, Aarathi P). It was originally created to empower and support beauty with natural products and later on it also started encompassing natural cosmetic products.

The plan was laid in the beginning of 2020 and was put into action in June 2020. The initial products introduced were only for skin care and later on they were amplified to cosmetic products.
